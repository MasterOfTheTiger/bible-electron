Translations

American Standard Version (ASV)

Offline Available

The American Standard Version (ASV) is a translation of the Bible from 1901.

Darby Bible (DBY)

Offline Available

The Darby Bible is a translation of the Bible from 1890 by John Nelson Darby for "unlearned who have neither access to manuscript texts nor training and knowledge of ancient languages of the Scriptures".

Jubilee 2000 Bible (JUB)

Offline Available

King James 2000 (KJ2000)

Offline Available

The King James 2000 translation is "a King James Version brought forward 400 years." It subsitutes old English words for modern English.

King James Version (KJV)

Offline Available

The King James Version (or Authorised Version) is a translation of the Bible from 1611 commisioned by King James I.

New Heart English Bible (NHEB)

Offline Available

"The goal of this work is to provide a modern and accurate English translation Bible based on the latest standard texts for the public domain."

New English Translation (NET)

Online Only

The New English Translation is a free, "completely new" online English translation of the Bible, "with 60,932 translators' notes" sponsored by the Biblical Studies Foundation and published by Biblical Studies Press. wikipedia

Revised Standard Version (RSV)

Offline Available

The Revised Standard Version is a translation written in easy to read English.

Webster (WBT)

Offline Available

The Webster Bible was translated by Noah Webster in 1833.

World English Bible (WEB)

Offline Available

The World English Bible is a revision of the American Standard Version in the Public Domain. Completed in 2000.

Young's Literal Translation (YLT)

Offline Available

The Young's Literal Translation is a translation of the Bible by Robert Young in 1862. It is a very literal translation of the text.
